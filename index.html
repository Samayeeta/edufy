About Edufy

Learning Disability is a matter of special concern to the student community because we often confuse a student's failure for a lack of intellect or indiscipline, when the true cause is often a deficiency in one or more of the ways the brain processes knowledge. Edufy is a global youth-led organization that uses games and fun worksheets to offer quality education to children with special needs. We want to achieve simplified education for all so that everyone receives the education they deserve.
